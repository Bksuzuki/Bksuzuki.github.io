Describe your design for your site’s shopping cart. That is, will it be a separate page that the user can view and edit, or will it be integrated into the product pages? If so, describe in detail how this will work on your site. Provide several examples of using the cart.

My cart will be a seperate page from the 3 different product pages. The user will be able to explore the product pages regardless of whether they are logged in and add it to a cart. If the user proceeds to login, then the items in their cart will be added to their previously saved cart. For example, if a user were to end a their shopping spree at 3 nixon watches and logout, and proceed to come back, not log in, and add 3 nixon watches then log in, the total will amount to 6. For new account members, the shopping cart will also transfer.
